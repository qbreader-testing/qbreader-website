    Question Reader

    QB Reader is a text-based quizbowl question reader. Play some questions on your own:

    Tossups Bonuses

    Or, join a multiplayer room to play against other people:

    Multiplayer

    Question Database

    QB Reader comes with a searchable database of questions which have been downloaded and parsed from https://quizbowlpackets.com/.

    Database

    The answerlines from this database have been aggregated into a series of frequency lists by category.

    Frequency List

    The questions are parsed using the Python or JavaScript packet parser.

    Sign up for an account to track your stats, view per-question stats, and play geoword. If you already have an account, login here. To find out more about what an account can do, check out the about page.
